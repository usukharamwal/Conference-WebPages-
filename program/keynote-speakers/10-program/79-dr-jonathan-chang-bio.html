Biography

Jonathan Chang is AVP of Business Operation Planning, also Information Security officer at Vanguard International Semiconductor Corporation (VIS). While semiconductor industry is one of most complex global manufacturing. He has been in semiconductor industry for 30 years in 200mm (ssmc, VIS), 300mm (tsmc, qimonda), 450mm (tsmc) wafer fabrication manufacturing system design and operation, also Semiconductor back-end manufacturing in Infineon, TI and TFME to establish overall manufacturing excellence capability in front-end and back-end operation of semiconductor industry. He has worked with great teams to achieve world-wide fastest manufacturing CT and high productivity improvement in semiconductor mass-production environment with several innovation in manufacturing management, engineering management in global modern semiconductor manufacturing.  Jonathan has more than 40 global patents and papers on the advanced manufacturing areas, including advanced process and equipment control, 300mm full-automation manufacturing design, implementation and intelligent manufacturing and management. He has jointly setup smart manufacturing lab in Xian Jiao-Tung University (China), as well as intelligent manufacturing and management lab in National Yang-Ming Chiao-Tung University (NYC). Meanwhile, he has been the chairman of IEEE P2671 standard. He also got the 2023 industrial engineering award from Chinese Institute of Industrial Engineers. His research interest is always in semiconductor operation excellence enabled with advanced manufacturing design and implementation. And it has been supported by Industry 3.0, Industry 4.0 and today AI 2.0. Following with progressive innovation in IT technology, he always fully utilizes the modern IT capability to transform semiconductor manufacturing and performance. And the final performance could be only implemented with integrated know-how from semiconductor manufacturing management theory and advanced IT technology.
