Important Dates

Abstract Submission Deadline: December 15th, 2024

Abstract Submission

Abstracts of 150-250 words are to be submitted through the online abstract submission system using the special Abstract Submission Form.

For Abstract Submission on EasyChair Platform

  • Login to https://easychair.org/my/conference?conf=faim2025 (or Register as a user in case this is your first-time use)
  • Go to “make a new submission” and choose track - “Abstract Submission"
  • Should select up to 3 appropriate topics for reviewing classification
  • Should click on "Submit" upon process completion

You can find a detailed guide on how to submit your abstract on this guide.

Co-Authorship

According to the FAIM advisory board decision, a maximum of 5 co-authorships in submitted papers is allowed, only one of them pertaining to “fast track journals”.
