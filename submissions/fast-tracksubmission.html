Fast-Track Journal Paper Submission


Important Dates

Fast-Track Submission Deadline: January 28th, 2025

For Fast-Track Journal Paper Submission on EasyChair Platform

  • Login to https://easychair.org/my/conference?conf=faim2025 (or Register as a user in case this is your first-time use)
  • Go to “make a new submission” and choose track - “Fast-Track Submission"
  • Should select up to 3 appropriate topics for reviewing classification
  • Should click on "Submit" upon process completion

You can find a detailed guide on how to submit your fast-track journal paper on this guide.

Fast-Track Journal Paper Submission

  • According to the FAIM Charter, a maximum of five co-authorship submissions are allowed per author.
  • Every paper has to be presented by one of its authors and no more than 2 papers can be presented by the same person.

  • Authors submitting full-length manuscripts for potential Fast-Track publication must clearly indicate the journal they wish to be considered for:

    • Robotics and Computer-Integrated Manufacturing (Elsevier)
    • International Journal of Advanced Manufacturing Technology (Springer)
    • Journal of Manufacturing Systems (SME, Elsevier)
  • The fast-track journal paper should follow the format required by the selected journal. Authors can skip the 8-page paper submission. However, if the full paper is not approved through the peer-review process, authors will be invited to submit a shorter version for inclusion in Lecture Notes in Mechanical Engineering (LNME).

All accepted paper presenters are requested to use the official conference presentation template.
Download Presentation Template (.pptx): Presentation Template.

Guidelines for Authors
  • The author can choose to work with Word or LaTeX, following the format of the selected journal template.
    • Journal of Manufacturing Systems - Guide for Authors
    • Robotics and Computer-Integrated Manufacturing - Guide for Authors
    • International Journal of Advanced Manufacturing Technology - Guide for Authors
  • For the first blind review, no author names, academic titles, or descriptions of academic positions should be included in the paper. After the paper is accepted, author information, affiliations, and email addresses will be allowed in the accepted paper.
  • Use title case for the paper title, capitalizing all main words.
  • The abstract should summarize the content of the paper, including its goal, scientific novelty, practical value, and main results, in 150-250 words.
  • Self-citations per paper are limited to a maximum of three.
  • The language of the paper must be English. Submissions in any other language will be rejected without review.
  • Plagiarism is strictly monitored and will be checked using Turnitin. Plagiarized papers will not be accepted for review.
