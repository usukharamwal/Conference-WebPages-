History

The FAIM conference series was established in 1991 by Professors Munir Ahmad of the University of Limerick, Ireland, and Bill Sullivan of Virginia Polytechnic Institute, USA. Since its start FAIM has been hosted by 26 universities across 14 countries in Europe, America, and Asia.
